The card game of tough decisions

How to Play
  • Cards numbered 3–35 are shuffled; 9 are removed unseen.
  • Each player starts with 11 chips.
  • On your turn: take the card (and all chips on it), or pay a chip to pass.
  • If you have no chips, you must take the card.
  • Runs of consecutive cards only count the lowest value.
  • Score = card values − chips. Lowest score wins!
